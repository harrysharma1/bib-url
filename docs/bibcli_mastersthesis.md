## bibcli mastersthesis

Generate @mastersthesis bibtex entry

### Synopsis

This will generate an @mastersthesis bibtex entry e.g.
	
	@mastersthesis{CitekeyMastersthesis,
  		author  = "Jian Tang",
  		title   = "Spin structure of the nucleon in the asymptotic limit",
  		school  = "Massachusetts Institute of Technology",
  		year    = 1996,
  		address = "Cambridge, MA",
  		month   = sep
	}
