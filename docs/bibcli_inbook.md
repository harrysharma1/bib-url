## bibcli inbook

Generate @inbook bibtex entry

### Synopsis

This will generate an @inbook bibtex entry e.g.
	
	@inbook{CitekeyInbook,
  		author    = "Lisa A. Urry and Michael L. Cain and Steven A. Wasserman and Peter V. Minorsky and Jane B. Reece",
  		title     = "Photosynthesis",
  		booktitle = "Campbell Biology",
  		year      = "2016",
  		publisher = "Pearson",
  		address   = "New York, NY",
  		pages     = "187--221"
	}
